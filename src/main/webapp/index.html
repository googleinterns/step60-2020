About Us

We created this webage as Google STEP interns so students going into university could see what college life is like!

We know transitioning into college can be difficult, especially in the middle of a pandemic.

We hope that our videos, blog posts, polls, and images help students understand a little bit more about college life, and prepare you for the start of your future!
